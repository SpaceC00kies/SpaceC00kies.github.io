      3 Days in Paris

      I felt like it was a vivid dream. Time went by really fast. I wish i was there with the person I love.


      Day 1

      I totally fell in love with desserts I found all over Paris. Specifically, macarons.
      I always seek for the best macarons back in my country where I live, and I thought
      I have found my favourite until I actually tried the one made fresh in its original location.

      Day 2

      Today I visited Paris gardens. All I can say is that it felt like I step
      into a magical realm fall out of disney movies. It felt as if all my stress
      has been released.

      Day 3

      Ah, Paris is also the home to world class luxury brand name. Although not much
      $$$ to spend, at least my eyes enjoy looking at it (and dream that I was able to
      purchase some of them).
